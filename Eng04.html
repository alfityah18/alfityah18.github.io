Twice the Spread of Corruption

وَقَضَيْنَآ إِلَىٰ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ فِى ٱلْكِتَٰبِ لَتُفْسِدُنَّ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ مَرَّتَيْنِ وَلَتَعْلُنَّ عُلُوًّا كَبِيرًا

And We conveyed to the Children of Israel in the Scripture (the Torah) that: “You will certainly cause corruption on the earth twice, and you will surely reach a degree of great haughtiness.”

فَإِذَا جَآءَ وَعْدُ أُولَىٰهُمَا بَعَثْنَا عَلَيْكُمْ عِبَادًا لَّنَآ أُو۟لِى بَأْسٍ شَدِيدٍ فَجَاسُوا۟ خِلَٰلَ ٱلدِّيَارِۚ وَكَانَ وَعْدًا مَّفْعُولًا

So, when the first of the two promise came (for Us to punish your transgressions), We sent against you some of Our servants of great military might, and they ravaged even into your homes. And this was a promise fulfilled.

ثُمَّ رَدَدْنَا لَكُمُ ٱلْكَرَّةَ عَلَيْهِمْ وَأَمْدَدْنَٰكُم بِأَمْوَٰلٍ وَبَنِينَ وَجَعَلْنَٰكُمْ أَكْثَرَ نَفِيرًا

Then (after your repentance), We gave back to you a return victory over them, and We reinforced you with wealth and offspring, and made you numerous in manpower.

إِنْ أَحْسَنتُمْ أَحْسَنتُمْ لِأَنفُسِكُمْۖ وَإِنْ أَسَأْتُمْ فَلَهَاۚ فَإِذَا جَآءَ وَعْدُ ٱلْءَاخِرَةِ لِيَسُۥٓـُٔوا۟ وُجُوهَكُمْ وَلِيَدْخُلُوا۟ ٱلْمَسْجِدَ كَمَا دَخَلُوهُ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةٍ وَلِيُتَبِّرُوا۟ مَا عَلَوْا۟ تَتْبِيرًا

If you act rightly, it is for your own good. But if you do wrong, it is your own loss. Then, when the final promise would come to pass (for Us to punish you once again), (We would send your enemies) to totally disgrace you and enter Al-Masjid (il-Aqsa) as (how the previous enemies) entered (Jerusalem) the first time. And they will utterly destroy whatever would fall into their hands, with total destruction.

عَسَىٰ رَبُّكُمْ أَن يَرْحَمَكُمْۚ وَإِنْ عُدتُّمْ عُدْنَاۘ وَجَعَلْنَا جَهَنَّمَ لِلْكَٰفِرِينَ حَصِيرًا

Perhaps your Lord will show you mercy. But if you return (to transgressions), We will return (to punishment), and We have made Hell a prison for the disbelievers.
