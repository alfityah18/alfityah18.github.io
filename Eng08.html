The three signs foretold by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ have long since been unfolding. Yet not everyone perceives them. This is because they emerge as Islam fades—when ignorance and sin have already veiled the sight of the heart, like a night cloaked in darkness. In Musannaf Ibn Abi Shaybah 37569, when asked about the most severe form of fitna, Hudhayfah ibn al-Yaman (RA) explained that:

أَنْ يُعْرَضَ عَلَيْكَ الْخَيْرُ وَالشَّرُّ لَا تَدْرِي أَيَّهُمَا تَتْبَعُ

“It is when both good and evil are laid before you, but you are unsure which path to follow.”

Mohd bin Mohd | Month 00, 2025 | 00 Month, 1447
