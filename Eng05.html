Contrasting Attributes: Khutaba vs Ulama

3. Knowledge

*The hadith was graded as Sahih/Authentic by Al-Albani (RH) in Al-Silsilah as-Sahihah 2510.

The second guidance relates to the Messenger ﷺ's emphasis on the proliferation of preachers compared to scholars at the twilight of times. Gaining insights from the utterance of ‘Ali (RA) in Sunan ad-Darimi 261 and Anas ibn Malik (RA) in Sahih al-Bukhari 7068, the ratio of preachers to scholars could potentially extend even to 90-10 percent. الله أعلم. Furthermore, this highlights their distinct roles, particularly in an era where societal shifts blur the lines between the two. However, this distinction does not negate the fact that some preachers do possess scholarly attributes. To understand the consequences of the abundance of preachers and scarcity of scholars on the ummah today, our thorough exploration begins with the origin of “الخطباء.” Join me as we travel back in time.

Mohd bin Mohd | September 12, 2023 | 26 Safar, 1445
