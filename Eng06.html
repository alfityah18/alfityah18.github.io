We often hear the phrase, “Patience is a part of faith.” This originates from the words of ‘Abdullah bin Mas’ud (RA) as recorded in Shu’ab al-Iman 9083, and authenticated by Ibn Hajar al-‘Asqalani (RH). However, the phrase “a part” can be misleading, as in English, it may imply an undefined portion rather than an equal division. The more accurate translation is “half.” The full hadith is as follows:

الصَّبْرُ نِصْفُ الإِيمَانِ وَالْيَقِينُ الإِيمَانُ كُلُّهُ

“Patience is half of faith, and certainty is faith in its entirety.”
